DOOR 64

the art of the cocktail

at DOOR 64, we believe the essential cocktail emerges from the harmony of premium spirits, masterful mixology, and the genuine connections formed between bartender and guest at our bar.

at the bar

signature cocktails

peach 75

our twist on the classic french 75. gin, peach puree, agave, and a splash of peach schnapps topped off with prosecco. served in a champagne flute.

midtown spritz

elderflower liqueur, fresh lime juice, club soda and prosecco. served over ice in a wine glass.

velvet room

smooth velvety cognac, orange liqueur, lemon and honey, shaken and poured over a big rock. served in a rocks glass.

walk it out

vodka, gin, rum, tequila, triple sec and blue curaçao with pineapple juice and a splash of sour. served in a tall glass.

otp water

tequila, fresh lime juice, splash of triple sec and sprite. served in a tall glass.

hartsfield in the am

for the folks with the early flights. sour and peach schnapps over ice topped with club soda. served in a tall glass.

lenox lemondrop

tequila, simple syrup, lemon juice and triple sec, shaken and served in a martini glass with a sugar rim.

stone mountain punch

dark rum or spiced rum with orange juice, pineapple juice and grenadine. served in a tall glass with lime garnish.

the 285'tini

citrus vodka, pomegranate juice, triple sec, fresh lime juice and a couple of dashes of orange bitters. shaken and served in a martini glass with a lemon twist.

old atlanta

our atl twist on the classic old fashion cocktail...a sweet tea old fashion. sweet tea, bourbon, splash of limoncello, peach bitters, agave. stirred in the rocks glass and served with a big rock. garnish with lemon twist and luxardo cherry.
